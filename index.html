FAQ

Common questions

Do I need council approval for a pergola in Melbourne?

Permit requirements may depend on your property, council, structure size and project details. MJD can discuss your project and recommend the next step.

Can I request a site inspection?

Yes. Send your details, suburb and preferred timing through the quote form and MJD can follow up to arrange an inspection.

Can gates be automated later?

Many gate setups can be reviewed for automation. A site inspection helps confirm space, power access and compatible hardware.
